Configure your device to use the Asius API for premium features like high quality video streaming, clip rendering, and 1TB storage.

## Prerequisites

- One of our forks installed (openpilot or sunnypilot)
- Active Asius subscription ($10/month, 2 week free trial)

## Step 1: Sign Up

Create an account at [connect.asius.ai](https://connect.asius.ai) and start your free trial.

## Step 2: Configure Device

After signing up, follow the instructions in connect.asius.ai to link your device to your account.

## What You Get

- High quality video streaming
- Video clip rendering
- 1TB upload capacity per device
- Route preview images
- No device blocking - use any device
