# Install as App (PWA)

Install Asius Connect as a native app on your phone for quick access to your openpilot device.

## Android

1. Open [connect.asius.ai](https://connect.asius.ai) in Chrome
2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right
3. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
4. Tap "Install" to confirm

## iOS

1. Open [connect.asius.ai](https://connect.asius.ai) in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add" to confirm
