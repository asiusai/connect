# Progressive Web App

Asius Connect can be installed as a PWA for a native app experience - works offline and launches from your home screen.

## Android

1. Open [comma.asius.ai](https://comma.asius.ai) in Chrome
2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right
3. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
4. Tap "Install" to confirm

## iOS

1. Open [comma.asius.ai](https://comma.asius.ai) in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add" to confirm
